Site Optics & Quality (SOQI)

The site optics are the availability to see what happens through the system of research down to the level of site at any moment in the running period. It requires the information technology deployment and the standards which support the aggregation and collection of the critical operational performance and compliance data.

The CTU Site Optics and Quality Informatics initiative is seeking in utilizing the performance data which is site-based and system-wide standards informatics and the functions which will be ensuring the interoperability of the clinical trials management systems, data-warehousing, source-based data capture and the analytics to promote quality, safety, data-accessibility, industry collaboration and operational excellence across the global clinical trials ecosystem through the open-access, shared information technology platform.
