Quality Assurance Systems (QASI)

The QASI addresses the systematic weaknesses in the approach of the quality approach of clinical trials and the development of drugs enterprise: silo mentality and silo thinking with not sharing of the information which is not related to quality.

Informed by the experience of other industries like the airline industry is that while utilizing the partner which is trusted like the case of air transport, IATA is a mechanism which is reliable for the development and success while implementing the culture of sharing of the quality data and the “error reports”. Air transport has been doing this for more than 70 years.
