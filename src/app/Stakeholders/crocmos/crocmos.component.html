CRO/SMOs

It is a shared system for the clinical research which facilitates most of the core process essential to SMOs and CROs alike:

  • Partnering with the clients to be financially innovative by piloting the alternative model on the research which is specific projects and programs.
  • Reducing the process costs and key operations of conducting the trials for the similar benefits of CROs and clients
  • Increased protection of liability which includes regulatory, malpractice and legal
  • Reduces turnovers of sites by the enhancing of professionalism and sustainability
  • Improvement of site monitoring and site qualification processes which includes strong informatics which are mapping abilities and facilities of the single sites, including the types of sites
  • Providing demonstration to the primary clients with the commitment to decrease the speed to market
  • Re-establishing the public trust and credibility with addressing regulatory, safety, performance and operational challenges, and while meeting the obligations of Corporate Social Responsibility
