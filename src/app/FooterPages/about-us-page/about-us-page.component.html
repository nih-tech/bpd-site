About us

CTU-NIH is designed to cater the global trends and the local needs of the organizations and individuals across Pakistan and around the globe. It has opened doors to the Clinical Trial Unit, it is a state of art facility which offers a full spectrum of the core and supporting Administrative service to the local researchers and the global organizations. Clinical Trial Unit is established under BPD, where the work will be conducted under investigator-initiated trials across a diverse range of therapeutic areas.
