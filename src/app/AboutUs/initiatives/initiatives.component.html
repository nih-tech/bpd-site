Vision

NIHCTU will be most reputed, focal point for innovative, quality, clinical research dedicated to improving health worldwide by enhancing quality and effectiveness of drug development.

Mission

Our mission is to actively collaborate and provide platform for multiple stakeholders including government organizations, non- profit organizations, companies and individuals from all facets of clinical research to work on a shared, safe high- performance clinical research. NIHCTU focus will be finding the most effective, innovative, and ethical means of building, implementing and maintaining an integrated global system for clinical research in a timely and cost- effective manner.
